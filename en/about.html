Company Info

Under the group of LiHsin Construction Firm which has 120-150 professional architect and civil engineering technicians, SKY PV Energy provides comprehensive construction services from land development, planning design and construction works to the system integration of building and ground PV power plants.

Taiwan, located in the tropical zone, is the best-suited area for PV installations. And Taiwan government is encouraging the development of renewable energy. A recent forecast of 2019 sees 1.5GW new PV installations in Taiwan, making Taiwan as top 10 markets in the world with a global market share of 1.6%.

Having more participate in solar installations not only increases domestic power sources, but also can provide a stable investment income for investors. It is expected to make solar generation more common in Taiwan, and resolve the shortfall in electric power, benefiting everyone.
